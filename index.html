About Me

Being surrounded and inspired by nerds and technology enthusiasts alike, I have gained a keen interest in technology myself which progressively directed my passion for web and application development.


During COVID-19 in 2020, It was unfortunate that I couldn't freely do what I have always loved to do such as going to the gym, play basketball, and going out to spontaneous night-outs with my friends; especially when everyone was strictly advised to stay indoors (how boring...)


So to try and stay entertained, I explored my way further into tech and found out about Web Design and Development. As an undergraduate Product Design student at the time, this topic appealed to me the most, so I had to give it a try.


Several hours of 'hello world' and inevitable errors later, I have gained a passion for Digital Design and Web Development and today I am seeking to pursue a career in Front-End Development.
